Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Lekhini account and all associated data at any time, directly from within the app.

How to delete your account

1
Open Lekhini and sign in Make sure you're signed in to the account you want to delete.
2
Go to your Profile Tap the Profile icon in the bottom navigation bar.
3
Open the menu Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner of your profile.
4
Tap "Delete Account" Read the confirmation message and confirm that you want to permanently delete your account.
This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your account and all your data cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes the following data from our servers:

Your profile — display name, username, bio, website, profile and cover photos
All quotes and poems you published
All drafts you saved
Your bookmarks
Your follow relationships (followers and following)
Your comments
Your likes
Your Firebase authentication account

Analytics and operational log data held by Firebase / Google may be retained for a period per Google's data retention policies.

Can't access the app?

If you're unable to sign in or access the app to delete your account, contact us directly and we'll delete your account and data manually. Please include the email address associated with your account.

Email us at docs.vishnushanmukha@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We'll process your request within 7 days.
