About Lekhini

Lekhini (లేఖిని) means "pen" in Telugu. It was built for writers who write because they must — not just to be seen.

Most writing and social apps optimize for engagement, follower counts, and likes. Lekhini takes the opposite approach: ink your thoughts first, share when you choose. The focus is on the craft of writing, not on performance metrics.

The app is in early release and growing with the community. Developed independently by Shanmukha Vishnu.
