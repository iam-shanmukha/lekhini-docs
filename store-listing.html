Full description

Shown on the store listing page under "About this app". Plain text, no HTML.

Lekhini (లేఖిని) means "pen" in Telugu. It is a journal-first writing app built for Telugu and English writers who write because they must — not just to be seen.

✦ QUOTE CARD BUILDER
Write your thoughts, poems, and quotes in a full-screen editor. Style them with beautiful fonts, wallpapers, gradients, and patterns. Pick a photo from Unsplash or your gallery.

✦ TELUGU & ENGLISH
Native support for Telugu script alongside English. Write in your language, your way.

✦ WRITE PRIVATELY. SHARE WHEN YOU CHOOSE.
Save drafts and write for yourself. Publish to the community feed only when you're ready. No pressure to share.

✦ EXPORT & SHARE
Export your quote card as a high-resolution image. Share it to WhatsApp, Instagram, or any app — or save to your gallery.

✦ SOCIAL — QUIETLY
Follow writers you love. Like and bookmark quotes. Leave comments. None of the vanity metrics front and center.

✦ DISCOVER WRITERS
Find quotes by tag or search writers by username. A feed that shows people you follow first.

✦ NO ADS. EVER.
Lekhini is completely ad-free. Your attention stays on your writing.

✦ FULL ACCOUNT CONTROL
Edit or delete your content at any time. Delete your account entirely from within the app.
